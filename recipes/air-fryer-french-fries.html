Air Fryer French Fries

Description

French Fries are the perfect companion to a burger, hot dog, or sandwich! This recipe will help you make the best french fries at home using nothing but your trusty air fryer!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb russet potatoes
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/8 tsp paprika

Instructions

  1. Scrub and clean the potatoes. Cut the potatoes into even 1/4-inch thick strips.
  2. Rinses the fries in cold water and thoroughly pat dry.
  3. Add the fries into a large bowl and drizzle with the oil and toss. Sprinkle seasonings over the fries and toss again.
  4. Transfer the fries into the air fryer basket and cook at 380 for 10 minutes. Toss the French fries and cook for an additional 6-10 minutes until the fries are golden and crispy. Serve warm with your favorite dipping sauce.
